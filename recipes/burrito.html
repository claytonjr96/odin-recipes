Burrito

Description

A burrito is easy to make. It is also one of the most satisfying and delicious meals from Mexico

Let's get down to business.

Ingreditents

  • Tortilla
  • Rice
  • Beans (Black or Pinto)
  • Cheese
  • Carnitas
  • Lettuce
  • Cilantro
  • Jalapenos
  • Red Onion
  • Sour Cream

Steps

  1. Steam Rice
  2. Prepare Beans
  3. Crisp the Tortilla
  4. Add the rice
  5. Add the beans
  6. Add the carnitas
  7. garnish with the rest
